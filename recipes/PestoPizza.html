Pesto Pizza

Step 1 Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Step 2 Spread pesto on pizza crust. Top with tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, red onions, artichoke hearts and feta cheese. Step 3 Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned.

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • ½ cup pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • ½ cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  • ½ small red onion, chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  1. Prep: 10 mins
  2. Cook: 10 mins
  3. otal: 20 mins
  4. Servings: 6
  5. Yield: 6 servings
